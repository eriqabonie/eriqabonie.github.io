About

I'm a freelance web developer based in Manchester, Uk with over 5 years experience. As an independent Web Developer, I try and work with local businesses where possible, but have worked and will continue to work with clients from across the globe. I strive to understand my clients needs and work hard to bring their visions into reality utilizing the best technologies and platforms to achieve the needed results. My focus is to complete projects on time and within budget. Every project is unique and will be tailored to your needs. I look forward to working with you.

As a WordPress Specialist my skill sets include hand coding websites from sratch using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript libraries such as JQuery and of course WordPress. I also use Bootstrap, Joomla, Wordpress and other CMS platforms. My methodology is building for "mobile first", ensuring the website is fully responsive and can be accessed via all device types. Web development is an ongoing learning opportunity so I invest time in keeping up with new technologies. I also have a network of developers with various expertise that I sometimes work with during larger projects. Get in touch and let's discuss our next project.
