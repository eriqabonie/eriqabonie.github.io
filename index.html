About

I have always be amused by the web and was self taught before enrolling into university to brush up on my skills. As an independent web developer, I try and work with local businesses where possible, but have worked and will continue to work with clients from across the globe. I strive to understand my clients needs and work hard to bring their visions into reality utilizing the best technologies and platforms to achieve the needed results. My focus is to complete projects on time and within budget. I look forward to our next project.

My skill sets include hand coding websites from sratch using HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript libraries such as JQuery. My methodology is building for Mobile First. This website is built using Bootstrap, however some of my other websites are built using Wordpress and Joomla CMS platforms. I invest time in keeping upto date by enrolling in online courses and seeking out best practices from my collegues, blog articles and many other sources. I don't know every language, or every platform. But I do have a desire to learn what I do not already know. I also have a network of developers with various expertise that I sometimes work with during larger projects. Get in touch and let's discuss our next project.
